About Us

We're a team of 7 high schoolers at Princeton High School, currently working to design, develop, build, and operate a new, custom unmanned aerial system.

Flight Readiness

PHSFlight's SUAS division (Princeton Tigers) has been developing their plane, SUASERv1, for months. After a long design and development process, SUASER is able to undertake automated takeoff, landing, and flight, with a reliable safety override for skilled manual pilots to take control at any point in the flight.
